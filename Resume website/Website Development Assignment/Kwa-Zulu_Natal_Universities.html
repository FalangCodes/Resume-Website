University of Kwa Zulu-Natal

ABOUT:

The University of KwaZulu-Natal is a leading institution of higher learning on the African continent.Boasting multiple campuses, UKZN is a residential, teaching and research-led university located in the picturesque province of KwaZulu-Natal. The University has a proud and rich heritage of academic excellence.

LOCATION:

The UKZN is a university with five campuses in the province of KwaZulu-Natal in South Africa.The university is geographically divided into five distinct campuses, which partially correspond to its managerial and academic divisions. The Edgewood campus is located in the Pinetown area of Durban.The Howard College campus is located in the suburb of Glenwood in Durban, and is the main campus of the UKZN. The Medical School campus is located in the Umbilo area of Durban.The Pietermaritzburg campus is located in the city of Pietermaritzburg.Westville campus, is located in the suburb of Westville in Durban.

APPLICATION DATES:

Applications are anticipated to be closed on 30 June for Medicine: and 30 September 2023 for all undergraduate programmes. However, please consult the University's website for accurate details.

REGISTRATION DATES

Registration Dates will start 21 January 2024 and will close 19 March 2024.

Durban University of Technology

ABOUT:

The Durban University of Technology (DUT) is a University in KwaZulu Natal, South Africa. It has five campuses in Durban, and two in Pietermaritzburg. In July 2019, approximately 33932 students were enrolled to study at DUT. The University is one of 5 technical institutions on the African continent to offer Doctoral Degrees.

LOCATION:

It is located in the beautiful cities of Durban and Pietermaritzburg (PMB). As a University of Technology, it prioritises the quality of teaching and learning by ensuring its academic staff possess the highest possible qualification that they can get

APPLICATION DATES:

Application Dates for 2024 at Durban University of Technology (DUT) will open from March 2024 and close on 30 September 2024.

REGISTRATION DATES

Mangosuthu University of Technology

ABOUT:

Mangosuthu University of Technology (MUT) is a university of technology situated in Umlazi near the city of Durban, South Africa, on a site overlooking the Indian Ocean. MUT is located in the academic hub in the eThekwini metropole. It is a residential university.

LOCATION:

The Mangosuthu University of Technology (MUT), named after Mangosuthu Buthelezi (founder of the Inkatha Freedom Party), is located on the coast just outside Durban.

APPLICATION DATES:

Apply to be a MUT student now ,2024 applications are now open at Mangosuthu University of Technology. Applications are to be done online.

REGISTRATION DATES

Registration for Mangosuthu University of Technology (MUT) students will run from Monday, 30 January until Friday, 10 February 2024. Successful applicants will receive an SMS of their confirmation.

University of Zululand

ABOUT:

University of Zululand is a leading university that thrives on quality and fosters collaborative and innovative cultures with its rural and urban campuses. The University of Zululand or UniZulu is the only comprehensive tertiary educational institution north of the Tugela River in KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa.

LOCATION:

The University of Zululand is a comprehensive university located in the province of KwaZulu-Natal in South Africa. The university has two main campuses both of which are located in the northern part of the province.The main campus of the University of Zululand is situated in the town of Kwadlangezwa, which is located approximately 15 kilometers north of the city of Empangeni.
