Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University


ABOUT:

Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University (SMU or MEDUNSA; Afrikaans: Die Mediese Universiteit van Suid-Afrika) is a university in Ga-Rankuwa, Gauteng Province, South Africa. Its current incarnation was formed on 1 January 2015. Previously it was known as Medical University of South Africa (MEDUNSA) and later as a campus of the University of Limpopo.

LOCATION:

Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University is located in Pretoria North. More info on location

APPLICATION DATES:

SMU Online Application 2024-2025 The Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University, SMU Online Application 2024-2025 Academic year is going to be open on 3 April 2023 for all Undergraduate and Postgraduate qualifications and close on 29 September 2023 for all Postgraduate Programmes. To consult with the latest prospectus, go to the university website.

REGISTRATION FEES:

SMU registration fees are R3000. Students are only required to make an initial payment towards the total cost to their tuition fees & student accommodation. To find out more about the registration fees, check out the univeristy website.

Tshwane University of Technology (TUT)


ABOUT:

Tshwane University of Technology (TUT; Afrikaans: Tshwane-Universiteit vir Tegnologie) is a higher education institution in South Africa that came into being through a merger of three technikons — Technikon Northern Gauteng, Technikon North-West and Technikon Pretoria.

LOCATION:

The Tshwane University of Technology's main office is in Pretoria, South Africa. Its campuses are located in Tshwane (Pretoria, Soshanguve and Ga-Rankuwa), Nelspruit, eMalahleni and Polokwane. Its campuses are called Ga-Rankuwa Campus, Soshanguve Campus, Nelspruit Campus, Polokwane Campus, eMalahleni Campus, Arcadia Campus, Arts Campus.

APPLICATION DATES:

Applications for admission to TUT Online 2024 open on 1 March 2023 and close on 30 September 2023 for admission for January 2024 (excluding international applications and specified programmes).To consult with the latest prospectus, go to the university website.

REGISTRATION FEES:

The Tshwane University of Technology (TUT) Registration Fee for 2024 is between R240 to R300. Following TUT registration, Students must pay the registration fee before applying to the Tshwane University of Technology. TUT Application fees will NOT be refunded. To find out more about theregistration fees, check out the university website.

University of Johannesburg (UJ)


ABOUT:

The University of Johannesburg (UJ) is a public university located in Johannesburg, South Africa. The University of Johannesburg came into existence on 1 January 2005 as the result of a merger between the Rand Afrikaans University (RAU), the Technikon Witwatersrand (TWR) and the Soweto and East Rand campuses of Vista University.

LOCATION:

The University of Johannesburg (UJ) is a public university located in Johannesburg, South Africa. More info on location.

APPLICATION DATES:

University of Johannesburg APPLICATION AND CLOSING DATE Applications open on 01 April of the year preceding the year of intended study and closes on 30 October 2023 at 12:00 noon. To consult with the latest prospectus, go to the university website.

REGISTRATION FEES:

Day students (tuition fees) – All students not residing in the residences – R3 870.00 Residence students (tuition fees and residence deposit) – Only students with acceptance letters from residences – R6 470.00 Residence students who are provisionally selected/waiting for a space in residence only pay: R3 870.00. To find out more about the registration fees, check out the university website.

University of South Africa (UNISA)


ABOUT:

The University of South Africa ( UNISA ), known colloquially as Unisa, is the largest university system in South Africa by enrollment. It attracts a third of all higher education students in South Africa.

LOCATION:

Unisa’s Muckleneuk Campus is located in Pretoria and is a major landmark of the capital city. It was in 1972 that Unisa moved into its new home on Muckleneuk Ridge having vacated the old quarters in central Pretoria.View on google maps.

University of Pretoria (UP)


ABOUT:

The University of Pretoria (UP) is one of Africa’s top universities and the largest contact university in South Africa. They produce socially impactful research to find solutions for the world’s most pressing issues. They have a high quality of teaching and learning in the classroom, online, or in communities.

LOCATION:

UP has seven beautiful campuses, each with its own unique character and purpose. The faculties of Veterinary Science, Health Science, and Education are customised to their purposes, self-contained on campuses at Onderstepoort, Prinshof and Groenkloof respectively. Our Hillcrest campus is home to all our sporting codes, professional and recreational sport teams, and sports science research facilities.More info on location.

APPLICATION DATES:

Applications for study at the University of Pretoria in 2024 are fully online. Applications for 2024 will open on 1 April 2023. Application for admission to undergraduate study programmes for both South African and international citizens close on specific dates in the year preceding the year of study. Vaal University of Technology (VUT)


ABOUT:

Vaal University of Technology (VUT)is a tertiary institution in South Africa. It attracts students from all over the country. It is one of the largest residential Universities of Technology, with about 25,000 students, 300 programs, all primarily taught in English.

ABOUT:

The University of the Witwatersrand, Johannesburg (/ v ə t ˈ v ɑː t ə s r ə n t /), is a multi-campus South African public research university situated in the northern areas of central Johannesburg.It is more commonly known as Wits University or Wits (/ v ə t s / or / v ɪ t s /).The university has its roots in the mining industry, as do Johannesburg and the Witwatersrand in general.

LOCATION:

The University of Witwatersrand (known as Wits) is located in Johannesburg, the vibrant, culturally diverse and leading commercial city on the Africa continent. More info on location.
