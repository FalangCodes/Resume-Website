Sol Plaatje University



ABOUT:

Sol Plaatje University, otherwise known as SPU is a 10-year old University in the heart of Northern Cape's capital, Kimberly. The university started with just 135 students and 10 years later it has just below 2,000 students who are only first years. SPU is gaining its traction in South Africa with students from every 1 of the 9 provinces. As of 2023, SPU has 4 main schools, (i.e facilities), namely; Natural and Applied Sciences, School of Education, School of Humanities, and School of Economic and Management Science. The university has 6 on-campus resisdences all located under 5km around the central campus. Moroka is the largest on-campus residence that houses the majority of the on-campus students.

LOCATION:

SPU has two campuses located in Kimberly with the main campus located on Scanlan street. The South Campus which houses sports facilities and on-campus residences is located just opposite North Cape Mall

APPLICATION DATES:

Applications for the 2024 academic year for SPU are currently open and will close NOVEMBER 30, 2023. Prospective and returning students are encouraged to apply right now to avoid losing their space for the following academic year. To consult with the latest prospectus, go to the university website.

REGISTRATION FEES

There is a flat rate of R5,000 or currency equivalent for tuition and an additional R5,000 for Accomodation. Students with bursaries must appload their proof of funding on the website's iEnabler system. Aprroved NSFAS students can register without paying the registration fees upfront

FOR MORE INFORMATION:

For detailed information about SPU, it is advised that you go to the school's website and do your own research. Should you wish to skip the research process and apply immediately, visit the iEnabler system .
