Free State University


ABOUT:

The university of Free State also known as UFS is a South African university that was started in 1904 as a college called Grey university college with six students in humanities. Today UFS is one of the most successful universities in South Africa, having seven faculties that include the faculty of Economics and Management Sciences, faculty of Education, faculty of Health Science, faculty of Law, faculty of Natural and Agricultural Sciences, faculty of humanities and the faculty of Theology

LOCATION:

The university of Free State is located in the Free State province where it has 3 campuses. The main campus is located in Bloemfontein, the South campus is in Ehrlich also in Bloemfontein and the last campus is located in QwaQwa.

APPLICATION DATE:

Applications are currently open at UFS for 2024, and include postgraduates and undergraduates.

REGISTRATION FEE:

The university of Free State does not have any registration fees.

MORE INFORMATION:

For more information please visit UFS' Website

Central University of Technology


ABOUT:

The Central University of Technology also known as CUT is a well known South African university. It was established as Technikon Free State in 1981 and had around 285 students who were enrolled in secretarial, art and design programmes. In 2004 the name was changed to Central University of Technology. Currently the university offers 5 faculties that include engineering, built environment and information technology, health and environmental sciences, humanities ,management sciences, teaching and learning.

LOCATION:

The Central University of Technology is located in the Free State province and the university has two campuses. The main campus is located in Bloemfontein and the other campus is located in Welkom.

APPLICATION DATE:

Applications for 2024 are currently open an will close on the 30th of September.

REGISTRATION FEE:

Currently the registration fee for students who will not live at school residence is R 1 800 and those who are accepted for school residence will pay R 12 000.

MORE INFORMATION:

For more information please visit CUT's Website

University of South Africa


ABOUT:

The University of South Africa also known as (UNISA) is one of the biggest universities and was founded in 1873 at Cape town. By then it was called the University of the Cape and Good Hope. Fast forward, the university has grown so much that it has campus around South Africa. This University offer faculties that include Accounting Science, Agricultural & Environmental Science, Economic & Management Sciences, Education, Human Science, Law, Science, Engineering & Technology. This university is well known for distance learning.

LOCATION:

The University of South Africa has a campus that is located in the Free State province. The campus is located in Bloemfontein.

APPLICATION DATE:

Applications for 2024 are currently not open.

REGISTRATION FEE:

Registration fees usually depend on what course you choose.

MORE INFORMATION:

For more information please visit UNISA's Website
