North West University



ABOUT:

North-West University is one of the largest universities in South Africa.The NWU is committed to functioning as a unitary, integrated, multi-campus university that enables equity,redress and globally competitive teaching and research across all three of their campuses.

LOCATION:

The North-West University is located on three campuses in Potchefstroom, Mahikeng and Vanderbijlpark in South Africa.

APPLICATION DATES:

Generally, applications at North-West University (NWU) will open from March 2023 and close on 30 September 2023. However, please consult the North-West University website university’s website for accurate details.

REGISTRATION FEES:

All students must be registered annually , more or less at the beginning of February and the payable amount is R2500.
