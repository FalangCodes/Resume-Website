Rhodes University


ABOUT:

Rhodes University, founded in 1904; the University has a well-established reputation for academic excellence. With just over 8200 students, Rhodes is a small University, which enjoys the distinction of having among the best undergraduate pass and graduation rates in South Africa, outstanding postgraduate success, rates, and the best research output per academic staff member.It has six faculties: Commerce, Education, Humanities, Law, Pharmacy and Science

LOCATION:

Rhodes University is a public research university located in Grahamstown in the Eastern Cape Province of South Africa.The university has a single campus located in Grahamstown.The campus is informally called "the Rodents".

APPLICATION DATES:

Applications for the 2024 academic year for Rhodes University are current open and will close NOVEMBER 30, 2023.Those who wish to Study at RU are encouraged to apply as soon as possible.

REGISTRATION FEES:

Initial applications for admission must be accompanied by a non refund¬able application fee of R100 for all applicants. Then a Tuition Acceptance Fee of R800 is payable on acceptance of an academic place at the University.For other fees click Payment of Fees.

Nelson Mandela University


ABOUT:

Nelson Mandela University was founded through a merger of three institutions in January 2005, but its history dates back to 1882, with the foundation of the Port Elizabeth Art School. The university was officially renamed Nelson Mandela University on 20 July 2017, the only university in the world to carry the name of Nelson Rolihlahla Mandela.Nelson Mandela University has a total of seven faculties which are Faculty of Arts, Faculty of Business and Economic Sciences, Faculty of Education, Faculty of Engineering, The Built Environment and Information Technology, , Faculty of Health Sciences, Faculty of Law and Faculty of Science.

LOCATION:

Nelson Mandela University has seven campuses, six in Gqeberha and one in George.The main campus of the university is the South Campus located in Summerstrand, Gqeberha. The other campuses are North Campus, Second Avenue Campus, Missionvale Campus, Ocean Sciences Campus and Bird Street Campus.

APPLICATION DATES:

The applications for Nelson Mandela University are now open and will close in September of 2023. To consult with the latest prospectus, go to the university website.

REGISTRATION FEES:

Online applications will be free of charge but registration fees will cost you R1500.For other fees click Student Accounts Guide.

University of Fort Hare


ABOUT:

The University of Fort Hare is a public university in Alice, Eastern Cape, South Africa which is also known as UFH. It has six faculties Education, Health Sciences, Law, Management & Commerce, Science & Agriculture, Social Sciences & Humanities all of which offer qualifications up to the doctoral level.

LOCATION:

The University of Fort Hare has three campuses, Alice which is the main campus with over 8000 registered students.Bhisho campus and East London campus being the other campuses.

APPLICATION DATES:

Applications for the 2024 academic year for UFH are currently open and will close in NOVEMBER of 2023. Prospective and returning students to encouraged to apply as soon as possible. To consult with the latest prospectus,to go to the university website.

Walter Sisulu University


ABOUT:

Walter Sisulu University (WSU) was formed in 2005 after the merger of Border Technikon, Eastern Cape Technikon and University of Transkei.The new university was named in honour of an icon of the South African liberation struggle, the late Walter Max Ulyate Sisulu.Walter Sisulu University has several faculties.The Faculty of Natural Sciences comprises the Department of Biological and Environmental Sciences, Mathematical Sciences and Computing as well as Chemical and Physical Sciences. The university also has faculties of Humanities, Education, Health Sciences, Business, Management Science and Law, and Science, Engineering and Technology courses

LOCATION:

Walter Sisulu University has four campuses, Mthatha which is the main campus then Butterworth, Buffalo City and Komani as the othe campuses.

APPLICATION DATES:

Applications for the 2024 academic year for WSU are currently open and will close in October of 2023. Those interested in studying at Walter Sisulu University are advised to apply as soon as possible to avoid losing their space for the following academic year. To consult with the latest prospectus, to go to the university website.
