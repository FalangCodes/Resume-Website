Why University Hub

University Hub provides students with comprehensive information about South African universities and the programs that they offer. The website also provides the location of the instituitions, application dates, registration fees and hyperlinks to the institution websites. Students can easily compare University courses, fees and career opportunities to find the right choice for their personal educational goals. The website is meant to be used as a comprehensive tool for students seeking information about our local public universities in South Africa.

About us:

University hub was created by the Mhudi Six non-profitable organization. The organization's goal is to provide students with accurate and up to date information about universities found in South Africa. By publishing this website, we hope to inform and help those in need of the information provided in the website.
