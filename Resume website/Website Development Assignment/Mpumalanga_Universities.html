University of Mpumalanga



ABOUT:

The University of Mpumalanga is a public university located in the province of Mpumalanga, South Africa.It is committed to developing as an African University providing leadership in knowledge creation and dissemination. UMP was established in 2013 and enrolled the first cohort of 169 students in February 2014. The university has three formally constituted faculties being the Faculty of Agriculture and Natural Sciences, the Faculty of Education, and the Faculty of Social Sciences and Humanities.

LOCATION:

The University of Mpumalanga (UMP) is situated in the scenic Province of the rising sun in the town of Nelspruit, a context for staff and students’ inspiration. The two varsity campuses are, Mbombela Campus located at Cnr R40 and D725 Roads Mbombela 1200 and Siyabuswa Campus located at Bheki Mfundo Drive Siyabuswa 0472.

APPLICATION DATES:

Applications for 2023 admissions open in June of 2023 then the closing dates are 30 September 2023 for Bachelor of Education in Foundation Phase Teaching. All other Undergraduate Programmes close in November of 2023. Honours Programmes, Advanced Diplomas and Postgraduate Diplomas close in November of 2023. Masters Programmes in January 2024.
